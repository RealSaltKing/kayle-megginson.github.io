  2. The cubic spline between 4 points will consist of 3 cubic functions, each of the form $S_i=a_ix^3+b_ix^2+c_ix+d_i$. Thus, your function needs to find the 12 uknown parameter values $a_1, b_1, \ldots, c_3, d_3$ from the user inputted 4 points $(x_0, y_0), (x_1, y_1), (x_2, y_2),$ and $(x_3, y_3)$.
